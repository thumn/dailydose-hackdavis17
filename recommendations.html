RECOMMENDATIONS

It's a great idea to develop healthy daily habits that will pay off for the YOU of the future! Here are a few ideas to promote your wellness day by day:

  • Paint a picture
  • Listen to a song you've never heard before
  • Breathe deeply for five minutes, concentrating on your breath
  • Make a new dish
  • Go to sleep at 9PM
  • Compliment a stranger
  • Compliment a friend or family member
  • Go stargazing
  • Take a walk through the park
  • Go on a morning hike
  • Get dressed up
  • Treat yourself to one nice meal
  • Try new food
